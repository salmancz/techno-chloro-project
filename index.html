About Us

We would like to introduce ourselves as a leading Engineering Firm specialising in the field of Chlorination Systems and Accessories used in Drinking Water, Cooling Water, Waste Water Treatment and other specialized applications for more than 27 years, since 1992.

A team of experienced and committed Professionals design, manufacture, install and commission Chlorination Systems and Accessories on a Turn-key Basis. Both Vacuum-Feed Type and Gravity-Feed Type of Gas Chlorination Systems with capacities ranging from 100 Gms/Hr to 250 Kgs/Hr can be provided of any model, with any kind of state- of- art Automation.
