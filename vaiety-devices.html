CHLORINE HANDLING, STORAGE, SAFETY, TESTING AND MONITORING DEVICES

Chlorine is highly hazardous and virulent in nature. Therefore, exceptional precaution is to be taken in areas where Chlorine is stored and handled. Exposure to even low concentration of Chlorine gas for extended periods can cause grave health problems to Plant personnel. Chlorine corrodes costly Plant Machinery resulting in wastage of time and money. Chlorine leak never gets arrested by itself, it only gets worse if not properly corrected in time.

The Chlorine Handling, Storage, Safety, Testing and Monitoring Devices are designed by experienced teams of Engineers to identify, to prevent and to contain most of the hazards related to chlorine usage in any Chlorination Plant. Thus improving safety to men and materials. Also, this facilitates ease of operation, reliability and longevity of the Plant machinery.
